  3. August 2021

    2. Projekt

    Watching the ship crush through the Arctic ice pack is a sight you'll never forget, made even more memorable by taking a helicopter flight for a thrilling aerial view of the ship and expansive Arctic Ocean. You can expect days, and hours,to present variable sailing conditions this far north. The crossing from Murmansk to the North Pole can take us anywhere from 5-8 days. This means you'll have plenty of time to get to know your shipmates, stare out at the horizon, grab some drinks at the bar or do some laps in our indoor pool.
